Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How far in advance should I book engagement catering in Miami?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for smaller events and 2-3 months for larger celebrations, especially during peak wedding season (November to February). However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability. Our Miami location allows for quick response times.

Do you provide catering for both home-based and venue-based engagement events in Miami?

Yes, absolutely! We cater to all types of venues in Miami including homes, beachfront locations, banquet halls, outdoor gardens, and destination engagement parties. Our team is experienced in adapting to different venue requirements throughout the Miami area.

Can you customize the menu according to our specific requirements and dietary restrictions?

Definitely! Menu customization is one of our specialties. We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, Jain, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements. We also create fusion menus and can include regional specialties or family recipes upon request. Our Miami kitchen is equipped to handle all types of dietary needs.

What is included in your catering packages for Miami events?

Our packages typically include food preparation and ingredients, professional serving staff, basic serving equipment (chafing dishes, plates, cutlery, glasses), setup and cleanup, and event coordination. Specific inclusions vary by package. Furniture, elaborate décor, and full bar setups can be arranged at additional cost. We work with trusted Miami vendors for any additional needs.

Do you provide tasting sessions before finalizing the menu?

Yes, we offer complimentary menu tasting for bookings above a certain guest count (usually 100+ guests). For smaller events, tasting sessions can be arranged at our Miami location at a nominal fee that is adjusted against your final bill.

What is your pricing structure for Miami engagement events?

Our pricing is based on several factors including guest count, menu selection, service requirements, and venue location within Miami. We offer per-plate pricing that typically ranges from $50-$200 per person depending on the package and customizations. We provide detailed quotations after understanding your specific needs and can work within your budget.
