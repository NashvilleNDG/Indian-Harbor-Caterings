Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What catering services do you offer for seminars and conferences in Fort Lauderdale?

We offer comprehensive catering services for seminars and conferences including breakfast service, morning and afternoon coffee breaks, buffet lunches, plated dinners, networking reception spreads, and custom refreshment stations. All services can be tailored to fit your event schedule and attendee needs.

How far in advance should I book catering for my seminar or conference in Fort Lauderdale?

For seminars and conferences, we recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for menu customization. For large conferences (200+ attendees), we suggest booking 6-8 weeks ahead. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability—contact us to discuss your timeline.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for conference attendees in Fort Lauderdale?

Absolutely! We excel at accommodating diverse dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, dairy-free, and nut-free options. When you provide us with dietary requirement information, we'll ensure appropriate menu items are clearly labeled and available for all attendees.

What is the minimum and maximum number of attendees you can cater for in Fort Lauderdale?

We cater to seminars and conferences of all sizes, from intimate workshops of 20-30 attendees to large-scale conferences with 500+ participants. Our team is equipped to scale our service appropriately, ensuring the same level of quality and attention to detail regardless of event size.

Do you provide serving staff and equipment for conference catering in Fort Lauderdale?

Yes! Our full-service catering includes professional serving staff, all necessary serving equipment, buffet setup, beverage stations, and complete cleanup. We handle everything so you can focus on your event content and attendee engagement. For drop-off service, we provide all equipment needed for self-service.

Can you coordinate with our event venue and schedule in Fort Lauderdale?

Absolutely! We work closely with event organizers and venue coordinators to ensure seamless integration with your seminar schedule. We coordinate meal timing, break service, and setup to align perfectly with your program, respecting your agenda and ensuring smooth transitions between sessions.
