Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What catering options do you offer for family gatherings?

We offer comprehensive catering services for family gatherings including buffet-style meals, plated dinners, interactive food stations, barbecue spreads, traditional Indian feasts, dessert tables, and beverage service. All services can be customized to match your family's preferences and the occasion you're celebrating.

How far in advance should I book catering for my family gathering?

For family gatherings, we recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for menu customization. For larger family reunions (100+ guests) or special occasions requiring extensive preparation, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability—contact us to discuss your timeline.

Can you accommodate different dietary needs for family members?

Absolutely! We excel at accommodating diverse dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, dairy-free, nut-free, and other special requirements. When you provide us with dietary information, we'll ensure appropriate menu items are clearly labeled and available for all your family members, ensuring everyone can enjoy the celebration together.

What size family gatherings can you cater?

We cater to family gatherings of all sizes, from intimate family dinners of 10-20 people to large family reunions with 200+ attendees. Whether it's a small holiday gathering or a grand multi-generational celebration, we ensure the same level of quality and attention to detail regardless of the number of guests.

Do you provide full-service catering including staff and setup for family gatherings?

Yes! Our full-service catering includes professional serving staff, complete setup of buffet tables or food stations, elegant table settings, beverage service, and complete cleanup. We handle everything so you can focus on spending quality time with your family. For smaller, more casual gatherings, we also offer drop-off service with all necessary equipment for self-service.

Can you incorporate traditional family recipes or cultural dishes?

Absolutely! We love incorporating traditional family recipes and cultural dishes that honor your heritage. If you have specific family recipes or traditional dishes you'd like included, we can work with you to recreate them authentically. Our chefs are experienced in various cuisines and can help bring your family's culinary traditions to life.

What areas do you serve for family gathering catering?

We proudly serve family gatherings throughout South Florida including Miami, Miami Beach, Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton, West Palm Beach, Hollywood, Aventura, and all surrounding areas. We regularly cater events at homes, community centers, parks, banquet halls, and unique venues across the region.
