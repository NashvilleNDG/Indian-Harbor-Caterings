Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What catering options do you offer for family gatherings in Miami?

We offer comprehensive catering services for family gatherings including buffet-style meals, plated dinners, interactive food stations, barbecue spreads, traditional Indian feasts, dessert tables, and beverage service. All services can be customized to match your family's preferences and the occasion you're celebrating. Our Miami location allows for quick response times and local coordination.

How far in advance should I book catering for my family gathering in Miami?

For family gatherings in Miami, we recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for menu customization. For larger family reunions (100+ guests) or special occasions requiring extensive preparation, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability—contact us to discuss your timeline. Our Miami team is flexible and responsive to your needs.

Can you accommodate different dietary needs for family members in Miami?

Absolutely! We excel at accommodating diverse dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, dairy-free, nut-free, and other special requirements. When you provide us with dietary information, we'll ensure appropriate menu items are clearly labeled and available for all your family members, ensuring everyone can enjoy the celebration together. Miami's diverse community requires this flexibility, and we deliver.

What size family gatherings can you cater in Miami?

We cater to family gatherings of all sizes in Miami, from intimate family dinners of 10-20 people to large family reunions with 200+ attendees. Whether it's a small holiday gathering or a grand multi-generational celebration, we ensure the same level of quality and attention to detail regardless of the number of guests. We regularly serve Miami's diverse event venues.

Do you provide full-service catering including staff and setup for family gatherings in Miami?

Yes! Our full-service catering includes professional serving staff, complete setup of buffet tables or food stations, elegant table settings, beverage service, and complete cleanup.
