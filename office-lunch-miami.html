Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What types of office lunch options do you offer in Miami?

We offer a wide variety of office lunch options including buffet-style service, boxed lunches, plated meals, and food stations. Our menus feature authentic Indian cuisine, fusion dishes, healthy options, and international favorites. All menus can be customized to suit your Miami team's preferences and dietary requirements.

How far in advance should I book office lunch catering in Miami?

For one-time events, we recommend booking at least 48-72 hours in advance. For regular weekly or daily office lunch service, we can set up a recurring schedule. Last-minute orders can often be accommodated based on availability—please contact us directly to discuss your needs.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies for office lunches?

Absolutely! We take dietary restrictions and allergies seriously. We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, dairy-free, nut-free, and other dietary needs. Please inform us of any specific requirements when placing your order, and we'll ensure safe and delicious options for all your team members.

What is the minimum order size for office lunches in Miami?

We typically require a minimum order of 10-15 people for office lunch catering. However, we're flexible and can work with smaller teams for regular orders. Contact us to discuss your specific needs, and we'll find a solution that works for your Miami office.

Do you provide serving staff and setup for office lunches in Miami?

Yes! We offer full-service options including professional serving staff, complete setup of buffet tables or food stations, and cleanup after the meal. For drop-off orders, we provide everything needed for self-service. We'll work with you to determine the best service style for your Miami office environment.

Can you set up a recurring office lunch program in Miami?

Absolutely! We offer flexible recurring office lunch programs tailored to your schedule—daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. We can rotate menus to keep meals exciting and offer volume discounts for regular orders. Our team will work with you to create a program that fits your budget and team preferences for your Miami office.
