Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What catering options do you offer for company anniversary celebrations?

We offer comprehensive catering services for company anniversaries including elegant cocktail receptions, formal plated dinners, sophisticated buffet spreads, dessert stations, and custom refreshment bars. All services can be tailored to reflect your company's brand and the significance of your milestone.

How far in advance should I book catering for my company anniversary?

For company anniversary celebrations, we recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for menu customization and theme coordination. For large anniversary events (200+ guests), we suggest booking 8-12 weeks ahead. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability—contact us to discuss your timeline.

Can you incorporate our company branding into the catering presentation?

Absolutely! We can incorporate your company's colors, logo, and anniversary theme into food displays, table settings, and presentation elements. We work closely with you to create a cohesive celebration that reflects your brand identity and honors your milestone achievement.

What is the typical guest count for company anniversary events?

Company anniversary celebrations vary widely in size, from intimate gatherings of 30-50 employees to grand celebrations with 500+ guests including employees, clients, partners, and stakeholders. We cater to events of all sizes, ensuring the same level of quality and attention to detail regardless of the number of attendees.

Do you provide full-service catering including staff and setup?

Yes! Our full-service catering includes professional serving staff, complete setup of buffet tables or food stations, elegant table settings, beverage service, and complete cleanup. We handle everything so you can focus on celebrating with your team and guests. For drop-off service, we provide all equipment needed for self-service.

Can you accommodate special dietary needs for anniversary guests?

Absolutely! We excel at accommodating diverse dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, dairy-free, and nut-free options. When you provide us with dietary requirement information, we'll ensure appropriate menu items are clearly labeled and available for all your guests, ensuring everyone can celebrate together.

What areas do you serve for company anniversary catering?

We proudly serve company anniversary celebrations throughout South Florida including Miami, Miami Beach, Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton, West Palm Beach, Hollywood, Aventura, and all surrounding areas. We regularly cater events at corporate facilities, hotels, banquet halls, and unique venues across the region.
