Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How far in advance should I book catering for my Mehendi event in Fort Lauderdale?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for smaller gatherings (under 100 guests) and 2-3 months ahead for larger events. Peak wedding season (October to March) books up quickly, so earlier booking ensures your preferred date and menu selections. Our Fort Lauderdale location allows for quick response times.

What is the minimum and maximum guest count you can accommodate?

We cater to Mehendi events of all sizes in Fort Lauderdale, from intimate gatherings of 25 guests to grand celebrations of 500+ guests. Our team is equipped to scale our service to match your event size perfectly.

Do you offer vegetarian and Jain food options?

Absolutely! We specialize in both vegetarian and Jain cuisine. Our chefs are well-versed in preparing authentic dishes that meet strict Jain dietary requirements (no onion, garlic, root vegetables). We can create an entirely Jain-compliant menu if needed. Our Fort Lauderdale kitchen is equipped to handle all types of dietary needs.

Can you set up live food counters at outdoor venues in Fort Lauderdale?

Yes, we regularly set up live counters at outdoor locations, beachfront venues, banquet halls, and even residential venues throughout Fort Lauderdale. We bring all necessary equipment, including generators if power sources are limited, to ensure hot, fresh food preparation on-site.

What are your most popular menu packages for Mehendi functions?

Our "Mehendi Masti" package featuring live chaat counters, assorted starters, and dessert stations is extremely popular. We also offer customizable packages where you can mix and match items. During consultation at our Fort Lauderdale location, we'll help you design a menu that fits your taste preferences and budget.

Do you provide serving staff and setup?

Yes, our full-service catering includes professional serving staff, complete setup of food stations and buffet tables, decorative serving arrangements, and cleanup after the event. Our Fort Lauderdale team is experienced and professional. You can focus on enjoying your celebration while we handle everything.

How does pricing work for Mehendi catering in Fort Lauderdale?

Pricing depends on several factors: guest count, menu selections, service style (buffet vs. passed appetizers), live counters, and venue requirements. We provide transparent, itemized quotes with no hidden charges. Contact us for a personalized estimate based on your specific needs for your Fort Lauderdale Mehendi event.
