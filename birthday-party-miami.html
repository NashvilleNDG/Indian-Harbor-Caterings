Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What catering options do you offer for birthday parties in Miami?

We offer comprehensive catering services for birthday parties including casual buffets, elegant plated dinners, themed food stations, custom birthday cakes, dessert tables, and beverage service. All services can be customized to match your birthday theme and guest preferences. Our Miami location allows for quick response times and local coordination.

How far in advance should I book catering for my birthday party in Miami?

For birthday parties in Miami, we recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for menu customization. For larger celebrations (100+ guests) or themed parties requiring special preparation, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability—contact us to discuss your timeline. Our Miami team is flexible and responsive to your needs.

Can you accommodate children's birthday parties with kid-friendly menu options in Miami?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating kid-friendly menus that appeal to children while still offering delicious options for adults. From fun finger foods and themed snacks to classic favorites, we can design a menu that makes both kids and parents happy. Our Miami team is experienced in family-friendly celebrations.

Do you provide birthday cakes and dessert options in Miami?

Yes! We offer custom birthday cakes, themed dessert stations, cupcakes, cookies, and a variety of sweet treats. We can work with you to create desserts that match your birthday theme, color scheme, and flavor preferences. Our dessert options are perfect for making your celebration extra special. Our Miami location allows for fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

What is the typical guest count for birthday party catering in Miami?

Birthday parties in Miami vary widely in size, from intimate family gatherings of 10-20 guests to large celebrations with 200+ attendees. We cater to events of all sizes, ensuring the same level of quality and attention to detail regardless of the number of guests. Do you provide full-service catering including staff and setup for birthday parties in Miami?
