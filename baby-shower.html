Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What catering options do you offer for baby showers?

We offer comprehensive catering services for baby showers including elegant brunch spreads, afternoon tea service, buffet-style meals, themed food stations, custom baby shower cakes, dessert tables, and beverage service. All services can be customized to match your baby shower theme and guest preferences.

How far in advance should I book catering for my baby shower?

For baby showers, we recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for menu customization. For larger celebrations (50+ guests) or themed parties requiring special preparation, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability—contact us to discuss your timeline.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for baby shower guests?

Absolutely! We excel at accommodating diverse dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, dairy-free, and nut-free options. We're also experienced in creating pregnancy-friendly menus. When you provide us with dietary requirement information, we'll ensure appropriate menu items are clearly labeled and available for all your guests.

Do you provide baby shower cakes and dessert options?

Yes! We offer custom baby shower cakes, themed dessert stations, cupcakes, cookies, and a variety of sweet treats. We can work with you to create desserts that match your baby shower theme, color scheme (pink, blue, or gender-neutral), and flavor preferences. Our dessert options are perfect for making your celebration extra special.

What is the typical guest count for baby shower catering?

Baby showers typically range from intimate gatherings of 15-30 guests to larger celebrations with 50-100 attendees. We cater to events of all sizes, ensuring the same level of quality and attention to detail regardless of the number of guests. Whether it's a cozy home celebration or a grand venue event, we'll make it memorable.

Do you provide full-service catering including staff and setup for baby showers?

Yes! Our full-service catering includes professional serving staff, complete setup of buffet tables or food stations, elegant table settings, beverage service, and complete cleanup. We handle everything so you can focus on celebrating with your guests. For smaller, more intimate baby showers, we also offer drop-off service with all necessary equipment for self-service.

What areas do you serve for baby shower catering?

We proudly serve baby shower celebrations throughout South Florida including Miami, Miami Beach, Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton, West Palm Beach, Hollywood, Aventura, and all surrounding areas. We regularly cater events at homes, community centers, banquet halls, and unique venues across the region.
